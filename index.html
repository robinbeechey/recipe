Fish Tacos

Why

Tacos are Great, they are a popular food you eat by
using your hands and then put them in the mouth
and enjoy a lovely meal for all the family trips.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bushel of tacos
  • 5 Cups of fish
  • 4 Cups of vegetables

Directions

  • Grab 5 cups of fish and throw them in the tacos
  • Grab 4 cups of vegetables and toss them in the tacos
  • Put them in the mouth of the beholder and enjoy!
